Sam Martin,
We are pleased to inform you that you may qualify for the Canada XYZ Benefit. This benefit provides financial support to Canadians with disabilities, at a time when every dollar counts. Eligibile adults who hold a Disability Tax Credit could receive up to $2,400 per year. Your benefit amount will depend on your income and that of your spouse or common law partner, if applicable.

We think you're eligible based on:

  • your age (18 to 64);
  • having a valid Disability Tax Credit; and
  • having filed your income tax return for the last year.

You are invited to apply for the Canada Disability Benefit.

What do you need to apply?

You need 3 things.

  1. Your Social Insurance Number (SIN).
  2. Your Application Code. Your code is 123456. It's valid until September 28, 2025, and is unique to you. You can still apply at any time without a code, but using this code makes it faster and easier.
  3. Your citizenship, immigration, or Indian Act status in Canada. No documents are needed for your application.

You can also update or add your direct deposit information if you choose.

How do you apply?

You have 6 options.

  • Online at Canada.ca/placeholder.
  • Call Service Canada at 1-800-123-4567. Press 2 to apply.
  • Use a teletypewriter (TTY) at 1-800-123-4567.
  • Use a sign language interpreteer to call the dedicated video relay service (VRS) line. To use the service, register at srvcanadavrs.ca.
  • Complete a printed application. You can print it online at Canada.ca/placeholder or at a Service Canada Centre.

To apply on behalf od someone as their legal representative, you can complete an online or printed application.

How will you know if you qualify?

After you apply, we'll mail you a letter within eight (8) weeks to let you know if you qualify for the Canada XYZ Benefit. If you qualify for a payment, the letter will include your payment date and amount.
